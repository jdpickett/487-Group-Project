# 487-Group-Project

## Project Vision

### Controls

We only support keyboard.

Player Controls:
- WASD for movement.
- Shift to move slowly.
- Space to shoot.

### Entities

Player:
- Each bullet takes a certain percentage of the enemies health.
- The player can take a higher percentage of the enemies hp per phase they progress through.
- Starts with 100hp.
- A normal speed and a slower speed (Press SHIFT).

Better Grunt:
- A gun that shoots towards the player.
- 10 hp
- Each bullet does 5 hp.

Grunt:
- A gun that shoots in random direction.
- It shoots 3 bullets at a time
- 5 hp
- Each bullet does 5hp.

Mid Boss:
- Takes up about half of the screen width.
- Fully automatic gun.
- Shoots at the current position.
- 100 hp.
- Each bullet does 10hp.

Final Boss:
- Take up most of the screen, like 3/4 of the screen width.
- A laser beam that is in a fixed position that spans the entire height of the scene.
- Laser beam is INSTANT death.
- 300 hp.

### Game Phases
- Phase 1

Just a bunch of regular ol' grunts.

- Phase 2

Introduce the Better Grunt, spawn a mix of regular grunts and better grunts.

- Phase 3

Spawn a mix of grunts and when the player kills all grunts, spawn the mid boss.

- Phase 4

Spawn a mix of grunts and when the player kills all grunts, spawn the final boss.
